About Me

Hey there - I'm Paul Shannon, a freelance writer currently based in Rochester, NY.

And when I was in high school, I fell in love with research.

I began my first research project in the summer of 2021 as part of an early-college course, and in this, I found a deep-rooted love for the environment.

My studies chiefly surrounded harmful algal blooms (HABs), an environmental problem prevalent on every single continent worldwide, disrupting any affected ecosystem significantly.

Despite this niche specialization, this project allowed me to take the first step of what would become a multi-year process to hone my abilities and begin freelance writing.

Since then, over the past four years, I have worked freelance in a wide variety of industries, including tech, A.I., activism, and self help, designing product descriptions, landing pages, manuscripts, and much more.

And I am confident I can help YOU realize your business's full potential.
